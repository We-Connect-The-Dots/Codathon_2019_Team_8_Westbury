Character traits can desribe a character. In the short story, "The Very Hungry Caterpillar" by Eric Carle, the caterpillar displays many character traits.

One trait the caterpillar displays is determination. On page 2, after eating an apple, the author states, "On Tuesday, he ate through 2 pears, but he was still hungry. This shows that even though he was shamed upon for doing so, he still decided to eat more food to satisfy his hunger

  • The caterpillar is determined
  • The caterpillar is hungry
  • The caterpillar eats food
